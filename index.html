On the left here is my recreation of the sidebar seen in the popular messaging app, Discord. Each bubble is its own community, and you can be a part of up to 100. As you can see by interacting with the slider below, it can become difficult to navigate using this sidebar if you're a part of lots of communities.



Flip the switch below to see my idea for an alternative.



Not very impressive, I know.
The sidebar can now be resized by the user. This allows them to adjust the amount of screen space taken up by the sidebar at any time, and find what works for them. this doesn't reduce clicks, but it reduces scrolling and also allows users to see when communities have new activity in them (I haven't implemented the "new message dot" on this page).
